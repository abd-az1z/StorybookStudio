🎨 Color Palette


Primary (Dark Blue)	primary	#27187E
Secondary (Medium Blue)	secondary	#758BFD
Light (Supporting Blue)	light	#AEB8FE
Background (Off White)	background	#F1F2F6
Accent (CTA Orange)	accent	#FF8600


⸻

🧩 UI Role Breakdown

🧱 1. Backgrounds

Main Page Background	#F1F2F6 (background)	Clean, bright, reduces eye strain
Cards / Sections	White or #AEB8FE (light)	Provides soft contrast
NavBar/Footer	#27187E (primary)	Strong, trustworthy; separates structure
Modal / Form BG	#F1F2F6 or White	Keep light for clarity and legibility


⸻

🖋️ 2. Text Colors


Headings / Primary Text	#27187E (primary)	Bold and readable
Subtext / Labels	#758BFD (secondary)	Lighter, softer focus
Placeholder Text	#AEB8FE (light)	Subtle and clean
Dark Mode Headings (Optional)	White on #27187E	Strong contrast


⸻

🔘 3. Buttons / CTAs


Primary CTA	#FF8600 (accent)	White	Slightly darker orange #e67600
Secondary	#27187E	White	#1e1469 or #758BFD
Disabled	#AEB8FE	White or Gray	No hover


⸻

📦 4. Borders, Dividers, UI Elements


Card borders	#AEB8FE or light gray	Subtle separation
Input borders	#758BFD (on focus)	Shows interactivity
Dividers	#AEB8FE	Keeps layout clean
Active state	#FF8600 underline or glow	Visual highlight


⸻

🟪 5. Highlights, Tags, or Badges


Status indicators	#FF8600 (for “new”, “AI”, etc.)
Info / Guide tooltips	#AEB8FE or #758BFD
Section labels / tabs	#758BFD (selected), #AEB8FE (unselected)


⸻

✨ Example Usage Flow (Landing Page)


Navbar	BG: #27187E, Text: #F1F2F6
Hero Section	BG: #F1F2F6, Heading: #27187E, Subtext: #758BFD, Button: #FF8600
Features Section	Card BG: White, Text: #27187E, Icons: #758BFD
Call to Action Section	BG: #27187E, Text: White, Button: #FF8600
Footer	BG: #27187E, Text: #AEB8FE


⸻

💡 Best Practice Tips
	•	Limit orange (#FF8600) to buttons and emphasis — it’s powerful and works best when used sparingly.
	•	Always maintain sufficient contrast — use tools like accessible-colors.com to check contrast ratios.
	•	Create elevation using shadows or layers rather than too many color blocks.
	•	Be consistent — if #27187E is heading text, don’t switch it randomly.
